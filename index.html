Little Bits about Us

This pixelated Poké-Gallery is an homage to the original Pokémon games released in the 1990s. On February 27th, 2023 we celebrated 27 years of Pocket Monsters. Released for Nintendo's very first handheld console, the Game Boy, we were introduced to our first generation of Pokémon, still in black and white, with the titles Blue and Red. We started our adventure with 151 catchable characters in the Kanto region and almost 30 years later we exceeded 1000 pint sized pals as we entered Paldea in 2022. Come and join us on our journey as we travel through the world of Pokémon.
